What we do not collect

Sharing and selling

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us run the Site (for example, email delivery and analytics), only as needed to provide those services.

Data storage and security

Email subscription data and analytics are stored and processed by third-party providers. We take reasonable measures designed to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
